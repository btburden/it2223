Favorite CD Releases

Doo-Wops & Hooligans (2010)
A stunning debut that blends pop, soul, and reggae. Tracks like "Just the Way You Are" and "Grenade" showcase Bruno’s vocal range and emotional depth.
Unorthodox Jukebox (2012)
This album dives deeper into funk and R&B. "Locked Out of Heaven" and "When I Was Your Man" are standout hits that reveal his versatility and retro flair.
24K Magic (2016)
A glittering, groove-heavy celebration of funk and swagger. "24K Magic" and "That's What I Like" are pure dance-floor gold, dripping with charisma and style.
